About Me

This page will serve as a personal introduction.

I developed my love for mathematics in college. As I completed my minor in Biology, I found a home at the intersection of math and other science. I discovered a passion for using my technical background to investigate the world through data analysis.

As I progressed in school, I learned that a LOT of math and statistics involves programming. The more I coded, the more I enjoyed learning about how codes work. In turn, I have fostered a new passion for programming and exploring the world of computer science.

In mathematics, finding solutions often involves combining different concepts like interlocking pieces in a puzzle. I take this problem-solving approach with me in my work, looking for creative, efficient, and comprehensible ways to complete tasks.
